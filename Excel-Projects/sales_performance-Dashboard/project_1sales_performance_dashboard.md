# Excel Project 1 - Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
- Tool Used: Microsoft Excel
- Dataset: Sample Sales Data
- Total Rows: 2823 orders
- Total Columns: 25
- Time Period: 2003 to 2005
- Status: Completed 

## Problem Statement
Analyze sales data of a company to find
key insights about products, customers,
territories and sales trends over 3 years.

## Steps Followed

### Step 1 - Data Cleaning
- Removed unnecessary columns
- Fixed invalid values in phone column
- Handled NULL values in address columns
- Verified sales data using calculated column
- Applied conditional formatting on sales column

### Step 2 - Pivot Tables Created
- Sales by Product Line
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Yearly Sales Comparison
- Territory wise Sales
- Deal Size breakdown
- Order Status breakdown
- Quarter wise Performance
- Top 10 Customers by Sales
- Average Price by Product Line
- Territory contribution to Sales

### Step 3 - Charts Created
- Bar Chart - Sales by Product Line
- Line Chart - Monthly Sales Trend
- Column Chart - Yearly Sales
- Pie Chart - Sales by Deal Size
- Pie Chart - Territory wise Sales
- Pie Chart - Order Status Breakdown
- Horizontal Bar - Top 10 Customers

### Step 4 - Dashboard Built
- Added 4 KPI cards at top
- Combined all charts on one sheet
- Added 4 interactive Slicers
- Applied professional formatting and titles

## Key Findings

1. Classic Cars generated the highest revenue
   among all product lines
2. November recorded highest sales
   every single year
3. 2004 was the best performing year
   out of 2003 2004 and 2005
4. EMEA territory contributed most
   to overall sales
5. Medium deal size was most common
   across all orders
6. 96% of all orders were successfully shipped
7. Top customer contributed significantly
   more than average customers

## Slicers Added
- YEAR ID - Filter by 2003 2004 2005
- DEAL SIZE - Filter by Large Medium Small
- TERRITORY - Filter by APAC EMEA Japan NA
- PRODUCT LINE - Filter by all product lines
